Point values
^^^^^^^^^^^^

- 1 point – A, E, I, O, U, L, N, S, T, R
- 2 points – D, G
- 3 points – B, C, M, P
- 4 points – F, H, V, W, Y
- 5 points – K
- 8 points – J, X
- 10 points – Q, Z
